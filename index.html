Nano-Mind!

What is Nano-Mind?

Nano-Mind is a custom Artificial Intelligence (like a mini ChatGPT) built completely from scratch!

Instead of needing massive, expensive supercomputers, this AI is designed to be super efficient. In fact, the live demo is running smoothly on a basic computer server with just 16 GB of RAM and 2 vCPUs!

It proves that you don't need giant tech-company resources or heavy graphics cards to build and run real AI.
